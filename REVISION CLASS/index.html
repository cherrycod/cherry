EARLY LIFE OF DESAI

desai comes from a family of kabaddi enthusiasts with his father and brother, Suraj also known to have played the sport.[4] While continuing to actively pursue kabaddi from a young age, Desai under the guidance of his parents and brother also completed his academics.
